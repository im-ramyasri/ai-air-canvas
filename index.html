What it does

Built with real AI

Every pixel is drawn by your finger. Every shape is recognized by geometry AI. Zero plugins, zero installs for the web version.

✋
Hand Tracking
MediaPipe detects 21 hand landmarks in real-time at 30fps with sub-20ms latency.
✏️
Air Drawing
Exponential smoothing on fingertip position eliminates jitter for clean, fluid strokes.
🔵
Shape AI
Contour circularity, vertex count and aspect ratio classify circles, stars, arrows and more.
🎨
Color Picker
8-color palette at the top of the canvas. Move your finger into the bar to switch instantly.
✌️
Erase Gesture
Peace sign (index + middle finger) activates a wide eraser. Natural and intuitive.
⚡
Gesture Debounce
Gestures must be held for 4 frames before triggering — prevents accidental switches.
How to use

Gesture Guide

Everything is controlled by your hand — no keyboard needed (except S to save).

☝️
Index Finger
Hold up only your index finger and move your hand to draw on the canvas.
Draw
✌️
Peace Sign
Index + middle fingers up. Move to erase parts of your drawing.
Erase
🖐️
Open Palm
Four or more fingers open — instantly wipes the entire canvas clean.
Clear
☝️
Top Bar
Point your index finger into the colored bar at the top to switch colors.
Color
⬆️
Lift Finger
Stop drawing mid-stroke — the AI analyses your stroke and labels the shape.
